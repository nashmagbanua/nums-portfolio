About NUMS: Streamlining Utility Management for Modern Manufacturing

The ABN NutriBev Utilities Management System (NUMS) is a comprehensive, centralized digital platform engineered to bring unparalleled efficiency and oversight to the critical utility operations within manufacturing plants. Designed to replace outdated manual processes, NUMS serves as the backbone for a smarter, more responsive utility management ecosystem.

Core Operations Streamlined by NUMS

NUMS meticulously streamlines essential operations including:

  • Power Logging: Real-time capture and analysis of power consumption data, enabling precise energy optimization and cost control.
  • Boiler Efficiency Tracking: Automated monitoring and logging of boiler parameters to ensure optimal performance, fuel efficiency, and regulatory compliance.
  • Chemical Monitoring: Precise management of chemical inventory, usage, and quality parameters for critical systems like cooling water, ensuring safety and efficiency.
  • Cooling Water System (CWS) Management: Comprehensive tracking of CWS parameters, fostering proactive maintenance and preventing system degradation.
  • Preventive Maintenance (PM) Work Orders: Centralized scheduling, assignment, and tracking of all maintenance tasks, improving asset reliability and reducing downtime.

Built for Security and Accessibility

At its core, NUMS prioritizes security and user-centric design. The system is built with robust role-based access, ensuring that each user interacts with data and functionalities relevant to their responsibilities: Admin, Mantech, Opscrew, and Guest. Secure login procedures safeguard sensitive operational data, and the system leverages a Firebase backend for scalable, real-time data synchronization and reliable performance.

Introducing Numi: Your AI-Powered Operational Assistant

Integral to the NUMS experience is Numi, our advanced AI assistant. Embedded directly within the platform, Numi is designed to be an intuitive, always-on resource. Fluent in both Tagalog and English, Numi offers voice and text-based assistance to answer operational questions and guide users.

Driving Digital Transformation

NUMS embodies a commitment to digital transformation in utility management. Our goal is to empower manufacturing plants with:

  • Operational Efficiency: Automating data collection, simplifying workflows, and reducing human error.
  • Real-time Decision Support: Providing immediate access to critical data and insights.
  • Enhanced Data Visibility: Centralizing all utility data for comprehensive analysis and reporting.
